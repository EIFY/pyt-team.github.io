Transform¶

Methods to lift a graph to a simplicial complex.

toponetx.transform.graph_to_simplicial_complex.graph_2_clique_complex(G, max_dim=None)[source]¶

Get the clique complex of a graph.

Parameters:

  • G (networkx graph) – Input graph.

  • max_dim (int, optional) – The max dimension of the cliques in the output clique complex. The default is None indicate max dimension.

Returns:

_ (SimplicialComplex) – The clique simplicial complex of dimension dim of the graph G.

toponetx.transform.graph_to_simplicial_complex.graph_2_neighbor_complex(G)[source]¶

Get the neighbor complex of a graph.

Parameters:

G (networkx graph) – Input graph.

Returns:

_ (SimplicialComplex) – The neighbor complex of the graph.

Notes

This type of simplicial complexes can have very large dimension ( dimension = max_i(len (G.neighbors(i))) ) and it is a function of the distribution of the valency of the graph.
